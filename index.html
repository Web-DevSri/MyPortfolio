About Me

I turn complex problems into clean, efficient code. With 4+ years of experience in JavaScript, Node.js, and Python, I love building products that are not just functional — but delightful to use. I’m passionate about open-source and mentoring junior devs.s I’m a full-stack developer who thrives on transforming complex, messy problems into clean, scalable, and elegant code. Over the past 4+ years, I’ve worked across the stack — from building responsive frontends with modern JavaScript frameworks, to architecting robust backend systems in Node.js and Python. But for me, development isn’t just about making things work — it’s about making them a joy to use. I obsess over performance, accessibility, and user experience — because the best code doesn’t just run well, it feels right to the people using it. Whether I’m optimizing load times, simplifying a confusing UI, or adding subtle micro-interactions — I’m always asking: “How can this be better for the human on the other side?” Beyond my day-to-day work, I’m deeply committed to giving back to the dev community. I actively contribute to open-source projects — because I believe great software should be shared, improved, and accessible to all. I also mentor junior developers, helping them navigate the early hurdles I once faced. There’s nothing more rewarding than watching someone grow from “I’m stuck” to “I built this — and it’s awesome.” When I’m not coding? You’ll find me exploring new tech stacks, writing dev tutorials, or geeking out over system design on a whiteboard. I believe curiosity is the engine of great engineering — and I’m always learning.
